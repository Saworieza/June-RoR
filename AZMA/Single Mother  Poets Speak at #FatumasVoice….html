Single Mother: Poets Speak at #FatumasVoice…

 

This Friday on #FatumasVoice
Single parenthood has been on the rise in recent years. With the escalation of divorce cases and eventualities such as death of a partner, more parents are finding themselves having to raise their children single-handedly. Remarrying is never a guarantee that one’s children will be happy with their step parent and things get worse when the relationship between the latter and the former does not seem to work.

In this instance, we shall focus on the realities of being a single mother in Kenya; the challenges and viability of them raising emotionally stable children.

Join us this Friday as we enjoy a debut poetry performance from Blusky PoeticBee Wambui, and sink in the thought provoking words of other great poets as well mellow voices from the awesome musicians lined up for the evening.

28.02.14
Pawa 254
4pm-8pm
